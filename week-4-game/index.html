Pokeball Collector!

How to play the game!


  1. A random number will be generated at the start of the game.
  2. There are four Pokeballs below.
    • Pokeball
    • Greatball
    • Ultraball
    • Masterball
  3. By clicking on one of the types of pokeballs, you will add a specific amount of points to your total score.
  4. You can only win if the amount of points you add up equals the randomly generated number.
  5. You lose if the numbers do not match up.
  6. You will have to figure out how many points each type of pokeball equals by pressing on the picture.
  7. Each time the game starts over it will randomly generate the values of each pokeball.
